Supporting Our People

Project information

  • Category: Embedded Wed Design
  • Client: Hillbrook School
  • Role: Front-End Developer
  • Technologies Used: Javascript, Html5 / Css3, jQuery
  • Project date: June, 2020
  • Project URL: Visit

Webpage for current and future students and their families about how the school is responding to COVID-19 in regards to how they will be supporting the students, the staff and the campus once the school re-opens.

I was given the information and stats of how the school was responding to the COVID-19 pandemic and was responsible for creating a responsive section of the webpage specified by the Director of Communications and Community Engagement. The client wanted boxes that displayed the specific stats and wording provided where the numbers would change (count up) when the user loaded the webpage. I was also responsible for adhering to the schools specific design system as well as making the page optimal for mobile devices.
